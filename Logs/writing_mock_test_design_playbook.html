Writing Mock Test Design Playbook

Tom tat kinh nghiem tu Writing Mock Test 01 de lan sau tao mock test Writing moi nhanh va dong bo.

Nguyen tac: Trang test la noi lam bai that, khong phai landing page. Giao dien can ro rang, it trang tri, uu tien prompt, bai viet, timer, word count va anti-cheat.

Khi tao test moi, copy test-01 thanh test-xx, doi data-test-id, prompt va metadata.

2. Overlay bat dau bai

  • 2 cot ngang: cot trai thong tin/quy dinh, cot phai dang nhap.
  • Khong bold qua nhieu; chi bold heading, label va nut chinh.
  • Anti-cheat nen song ngu Viet/Anh.
  • Guest Mode phai noi ro: lam duoc nhung khong gui ket qua ve giao vien.
  • Logo lay tu folder Logos, copy vao assets/images.

3. Layout bai test

  • Header tren cung: logo, save status, violation count, timer, submit.
  • Ben trai: de bai. Ben phai: textarea.
  • O giua co thanh keo chia be ngang, gioi han khoang 34%-66%.
  • Visual Task 1 gioi han theo chieu cao viewport de hoc sinh it phai scroll prompt.

4. Format Part 1/Part 2

Part 1

Part 1
You should spend about 20 minutes on this task. Write at least 150 words.

[Task prompt]

Summarise the information by selecting and reporting the main features, and make comparisons where relevant.

Part 2

Part 2
You should spend about 40 minutes on this task. Write at least 250 words.

Write about the following topic:

[Essay question]

Give reasons for your answer and include any relevant examples from your own knowledge or experience.

5. Timer va submit

  • 60 phut tong thoi gian.
  • 20 phut: timer nhap nhay va beep.
  • 40/55 phut: canh bao thoi gian.
  • 60 phut: auto submit.
  • Het gio hien tai nhung YouTube sound Qt02LiZdEAg, sau nen thay bang mp3 local.

6. Anti-cheat UX

Ghi nhan fullscreen exit, tab switch, window blur, paste/copy/cut, right click, page unload. Moi vi pham hien modal giua man hinh, blur nen, song ngu. Du 5 lan thi auto submit voi ly do:

Tu dong nop bai do vi pham noi quy 5 lan

7. Receipt va payload

  • Receipt hien: Ma de, ho ten, email, ly do nop, word count, so vi pham.
  • Khong hien Attempt ID va Google Sheet status cho hoc sinh.
  • Back button tro ve index.html cua chinh test de hien overlay ban dau.

8. Google Sheet

  • Student_List: cot A ho ten, B email, F tinh trang, G mat khau.
  • Writing_Answer: email, ho ten, Task 1, Task 2, Ma de, Ly do nop bai, Attempt ID.
  • Cheat_Writing: email, loai cheat, ngay/gio, ma de, Attempt ID, elapsed, chi tiet.

9. Attempt ID va ma de

Ma de la test_id/test_code, vi du writing_mock_test_01. Attempt ID la ma duy nhat cua mot luot lam bai, dung de noi bai voi cheat log va dedupe; khong hien cho hoc sinh.

10. Checklist tao test moi

  1. Copy test-01 thanh test-xx.
  2. Doi data-test-id, test_id, title.
  3. Doi Task 1 prompt/image/chart.
  4. Doi Task 2 prompt.
  5. Kiem tra format Part 1/Part 2.
  6. Test Guest Mode, login, word count, submit thu cong.
  7. Test auto submit het gio va auto submit 5 vi pham.
  8. Kiem tra Google Sheet ghi dung.
  9. Push GitHub va test GitHub Pages.
