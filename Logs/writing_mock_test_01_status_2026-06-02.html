Anti-cheat

Ghi nhan: fullscreen exit, tab switch, window blur, paste/copy/cut, right click, page unload. Khi vi pham hien modal giua man hinh, blur nen, song ngu. Du 5 lan thi tu dong nop bai voi ly do: Tu dong nop bai do vi pham noi quy 5 lan.

Google Sheet va GAS

  • Student_List: cot A ho ten, cot B email, cot F tinh trang, cot G mat khau.
  • Writing_Answer: email, ho ten, Task 1, Task 2, ma de, ly do nop, Attempt ID.
  • Cheat_Writing: log anti-cheat.

Can redeploy GAS moi de co auth_token, version trong health, va cac cot moi.

Attempt ID

Attempt ID la ma duy nhat cua mot luot lam bai, khong phai ma de. Dung de noi bai nop voi cheat log, dedupe submit va audit noi bo. Khong hien cho hoc sinh.

Chong spam

Dang co: Student_List, Guest Mode khong ghi sheet, auth token trong GAS moi, dedupe Attempt ID. Sau nay can them session server-side, rate limit, password hash trong database va captcha nhe neu can.
